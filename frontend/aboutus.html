Why We Built This

We recognized how fragmented and time-consuming property searches and registrations had become. By leveraging AI and VR, we aim to streamline the entire journey—from search to purchase—into one seamless platform.

Designed for Everyone

  • Intuitive interface—no steep learning curve
  • Smart search by location, property type, and budget
  • Step-by-step registration wizard with AI auto-fill
  • Embedded VR previews—no extra downloads required

Quick Demo

1. Search “Pune” + “Villa”
2. Browse listings & view VR tours
3. Click “Register” and fill details (AI helps)
4. Verify documents via AI scanner
5. Receive PDF summary & track status
